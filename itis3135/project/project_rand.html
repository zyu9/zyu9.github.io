  1. Project overview
    • Describe your application and its purpose.

      My applicaiton is an online market. It will contains different categories of merchandises, such as seafood, grocery, etc. Its purpose is to provide convenient shopping for everyone.

    • Intended users of the website.

      Anyone who is interested in our goods can become our user.

    • Overview of the content of the website.

      There will have an account system for customers manage their shopping cart. We will have a search bar for users to find what they want. We will provide a detailed order sheet for our customer. Users may also use coupons or discounts.
