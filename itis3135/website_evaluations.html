Website Evaluations

Simply Recipes

Simply Recipes is a website for home cooks, containing more than 3,000 reliable recipes. Have over 15 million people each month visiting their website. Simply Recipes was founded in 2003 by Elise Bauer as a home cooking blog to record her favorite family recipes. In 2020, Simply Recipes was acquired by Dotdash, a New York-based media company.


  • Contrast: This website did great job in contrast. It uses a light green blue color as background and uses a mainly black color(some deep green) as font. Makes information very clear and easy to read.
  • Repetition: It mainly uses 2 fonts, creates harmony and unity. Makes information easy to read and user-friendly.
  • Alignment: The overall layout is pretty. The website contains lots of pictures which are align nicely.
  • Proximity: The website makes related elements together as a section. Such as "30 Minute Dinners" section only contains dinner recipes.


Star Angel Bengals

Star Angel Bengals is a breeder of Bengal cats. Their kittens are TICA and CFA registered. And they are been awarded TICA cattery of excellence.


  • Contrast: The website's background is blue, fonts are red and yellow. This combination makes user difficult to read.
  • Repetition: The website mainly use one font. Title and intro are another font. But these fonts seem to be very common ones(Arial, sans-serif).
  • Alignment: All texts are aligned at the center. Makes it hard to read.
  • Proximity: Information are here and there. Makes it little bit difficult to find at first.
